About SmartGroc

Our Mission

SmartGroc aims to make grocery shopping smarter and more convenient for everyone. By combining intuitive tracking, expiry alerts, and AI-based recommendations, we help users save time, reduce waste, and make better purchasing decisions.

Features

- Track your groceries, quantities, and expiry dates easily.

- Get smart suggestions for substitutes, deals, and offers.

- Compare prices across stores for smarter shopping decisions.

Our Vision

We envision a world where grocery management is seamless, sustainable, and cost-effective, allowing users to focus on what matters most: healthy living and smart choices.
