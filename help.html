Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a grocery item?

Go to the “My Groceries” page, fill in the item details, and click “Add Item”.

Can I track expiry dates?

Yes! Each grocery item has an expiry date field. You will receive alerts before expiry.

How do I compare prices?

Go to the “Shopping / Price Compare” page to see store-wise prices and best deals.

How do I contact support?

You can use the Contact Us page to send your query to our team.
